Universidad de los Andes
Thesis project

The game consists of a simulation where the player takes the place of a farmer who must try to repair his land due to the contamination caused by industrial waste. The simulation runs through a field that is divided into quadrants that the player must repair through 3 games that allow him to recreate each of the treatments for the symptoms mentioned above.

The player has nearby elements with which they must interact in a specific order to successfully overcome the simulation. The game map has a field that is divided into 15 quadrants that the player must partially repair in each of the 3 games so that in the end, it is completely healthy.





If you have an Oculust Rift device, you can get the game from this link: Untitled Farmer Game



Universidad de los Andes
24 hour GameJam

Due to an unfortunate accident, you have fallen on a planet whose atmosphere has lethal radiation for you. Your suit resists and keeps you protected, but your oxygen is limited, look for tanks to supply you. Your partner who came with you in one of the ships is lost and you don't know where he is, FIND HIM before your oxygen level runs out. If you see a cloud of smoke, DO NOT CROSS THROUGH IT, there is so much radiation in that area that not even your suit can protect you.

A one level adventure developed by 4 people during the 24 hour gameJam organized by GameDev Uniandes in 2017. This was my very first videogame ever made and it can be found here: U-235
